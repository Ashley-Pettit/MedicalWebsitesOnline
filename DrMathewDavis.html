About

Dr Mathew Davis is an orthopaedic spine surgeon trained in Queensland with a spine fellowship at Mater Misericordiae University Hospital / National Spinal Injuries Unit in Dublin. He is registered in Australia and Ireland and has a keen interest in research and education.

Interests
Degenerative spine, radiculopathy, spinal stenosis, deformity, trauma.
Approach
Clear communication, shared decision‑making, balanced operative/non-operative treatments

Areas of Interest

  • Robotic and navigated surgery
  • Spinal trauma and tumours
  • Degenerative spine disease
  • Reducing recovery time through minimally invasive surgery

Memberships

  • Royal Australasian College of Surgeons (RACS)
  • North American Spine Society (NASS)

Qualifications
